Nat Klarsfeld began in 1927, primarily as a workshop collaborating with the big 5th Avenue stores in New York City in producing their fine jewelry, often worn by various celebrities and Hollywood stars.

Over the years the company became known in the market for our innovative ideas in manufacturing to achieve the highest quality standards.

Since 1927, we have been independently designing and manufacturing our original ideas and offering them to high-end stores across the United States with great success.

In our effort to offer the finest diamonds at the best prices, in 1994 we began directly importing our diamonds from cutting factories world-wide.

Our experience in design and intricate mechanics allows us to produce the finest jewelry in the market today.

We welcome you to experience our work and be a part of the Klarsfeld family.
